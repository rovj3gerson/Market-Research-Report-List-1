<p><p>The mechanical ventilation system market is categorized into three main types: ceiling-mount, wall-mount, and cabinet-mount systems. Ceiling-mount systems are designed to provide air circulation from overhead, ideal for large spaces. Wall-mount systems, installed at various heights on walls, are suitable for localized ventilation and often enhance aesthetics. Cabinet-mount systems are compact and designed to fit within cabinetry, making them ideal for smaller rooms or spaces where minimal visibility is desired while still ensuring effective air distribution.</p></p>
