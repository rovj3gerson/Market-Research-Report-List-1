<p><p>Hyperspectral Imaging (HSI) captures a wide range of wavelengths beyond the visible spectrum, enabling detailed material analysis. The market is segmented into various types: </p><p>- **Visible/Near Infrared (VNIR)** focuses on the 400-1000 nm range, commonly used in agriculture and environmental monitoring. </p><p>- **Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR)** covers 1000-2500 nm, useful for detecting moisture and chemical compositions.</p><p>- **Medium-Wave Infrared (MWIR)** spans 3-5 µm, primarily in military and surveillance applications. </p><p>- **Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR)** extends from 8-12 µm, essential for thermal imaging. </p><p>- **Other markets** include specialized applications, such as geological studies and food safety.</p></p>
