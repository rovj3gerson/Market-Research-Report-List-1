<p><p>The OPF CMOS sensors market consists primarily of two main types: linear image sensors and area image sensors. Linear image sensors capture images in a single row, scanning objects to produce a continuous image, making them ideal for applications like barcode scanning and industrial inspection. In contrast, area image sensors capture entire images simultaneously in a two-dimensional array, commonly used in digital cameras and smartphones. Both types serve distinct applications, driving innovation and growth in the imaging technology sector.</p></p>
