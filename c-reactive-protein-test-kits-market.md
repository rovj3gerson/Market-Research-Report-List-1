<p><p>C-Reactive Protein (CRP) test kits are essential for assessing inflammation in the body. The market is segmented into several types, including immunoturbidimetric, ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay), and CLIA (Chemiluminescent Immunoassay). Immunoturbidimetric methods measure the cloudiness caused by CRP-antibody complexes. ELISA utilizes enzyme-linked antibodies for quantification, offering high sensitivity. CLIA employs chemiluminescent labels for rapid results and accuracy. Other market types may include rapid test strips and point-of-care devices, catering to various healthcare settings and needs.</p></p>
