<p><p>The PE cling film market is primarily categorized into three types based on the polyethylene variant used: LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene), and HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene). LDPE is known for its flexibility and clarity, making it suitable for food packaging. LLDPE offers enhanced tensile strength and puncture resistance, appealing for industrial applications. HDPE, being thicker and more rigid, provides superior barrier properties, often used in heavy-duty applications. Each type caters to specific packaging requirements across various industries.</p></p>
