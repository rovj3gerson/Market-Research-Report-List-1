<p><strong>The iToF Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>cwToF</li><li>gToF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The iToF sensor market consists of three main types: cwToF, gToF, and iToF. cwToF, or continuous wave Time of Flight, uses a continuous light source to measure distances, ideal for short-range applications. gToF, or gated Time of Flight, captures distance information through light pulses, optimizing accuracy over longer ranges. iToF, or indirect Time of Flight, combines elements of both technologies, allowing for precise depth sensing in various applications, such as robotics, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
