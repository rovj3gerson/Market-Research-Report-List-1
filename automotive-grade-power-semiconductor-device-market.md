<p><p>The automotive grade power semiconductor device market encompasses several key types, each serving specific applications. Diodes/rectifiers convert alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC). Thyristors are used for controlling high power, often in motor drives. MOSFETs provide efficient switching for power regulation in electric vehicles. IGBTs combine the benefits of MOSFETs and bipolar transistors, ideal for high-voltage applications. BJTs, while less common, are employed in certain analog applications due to their linear characteristics and high gain.</p></p>
