<p><p>The construction market for bearings primarily includes ball bearings and roll bearings, each serving distinct functions. Ball bearings, characterized by their spherical rolling elements, provide low friction and support radial and axial loads, making them ideal for high-speed applications. Conversely, roll bearings, which encompass various types like cylindrical and tapered roller bearings, excel in handling heavy loads and provide greater durability in demanding environments. Both types are essential for ensuring the reliability and efficiency of construction machinery and equipment.</p></p>
