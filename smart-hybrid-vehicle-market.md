<p><strong>The Smart Hybrid Vehicle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Belt-integrated Starter Generator</li><li>Crankshaft-integrated Motor Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Hybrid Vehicle market is primarily categorized into two types: Belt-integrated Starter Generator (BISG) and Crankshaft-integrated Motor Generator (CIMG). BISG systems use a belt-driven mechanism to enable engine start-stop functionality, improve fuel efficiency, and provide additional power during acceleration. In contrast, CIMG systems are integrated directly with the crankshaft, enhancing torque delivery and enabling regenerative braking. Both technologies contribute to superior performance and reduced emissions in hybrid vehicles, catering to the growing demand for sustainable automotive solutions.</p></p>
